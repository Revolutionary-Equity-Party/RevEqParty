~Our Organization~

The Revolutionary Equity Party is a organization focused on the education and organization of currently unenfranchized people into revolutionary and antifascist mass organization. We lead a weekly study group focused on teaching the basics of Marxist and anarchist revolutionary theory and practice. Some of our party's goals include:

  • Demystifying newer folks of the left.
  • Turning broad ideals into specific actions to change the current state of things.
  • Creating class consciousness in liberal spaces.
  • Providing a platform to voice the opinion of the people.
~Our Program~

Revolutionary Equity Part, as a progressive organization; the following three values are the core of our membership:

  • Anti-capitalism
  • Anti-imperialism
  • Anti-prejudice
